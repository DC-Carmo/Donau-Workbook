Defensive System

Select side & zone

Mindset - Total Commitment

  • Workrate - relentless + discipline
  • Inside the ball - Hunt Hard / Concrete
  • On the ball - Accelerate through contact
  • Outside the ball - Square up
  • Violence at contact - bodies in front
Line Principles - 13 On Feet / 2 @ Breakdown
AXE
1st tackler - low chop, dominant tackle. Roll away if non-dominant with ruck.
HUNT
Inside defender - attack the ball if space; fight to slow ball if bodies present.
DOUBLE HUNT
2nd inside defender at the tackle - reads breakdown, decides counter ruck.
COP
Outside the Axe - holds width, organises fold, 2nd tackler if overs run.

Coach Introduction
Hi, I'm Mato
Quick Facts
Coaching Experience

I have worked with refugee communities and in the favelas of Brazil, using sport as a tool for development and inclusion.

I have been coaching at Donau for nearly a decade across multiple age groups. Currently coaching the Second and Third teams, the U18 squad, and Head Coach of the U18 National Team.

What Being a Pirate Means to Me

Being a Pirate means being part of a family. For a period of my life, this club was not only my team, but also my home. For that, I will always be deeply grateful.

Coaching Philosophy
All sports involve teamwork, but rugby is built on it. Teamwork is the essence of the game.
My goal is to create an environment where every player:
  • feels a strong sense of purpose
  • understands their role
  • supports one another
  • grows together as a team
Philosophy & Core Beliefs
